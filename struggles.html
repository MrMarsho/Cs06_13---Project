Scholar Struggles

It's okay to struggle. We are with you on this one.

Here are some common struggles students face:

  • Overwhelming tests and requirements
  • Intense procrastination
  • Bad company
  • Stress from other sources such as parents or teachers

Frequently Asked Questions

How can I manage my time better?
Try creating a schedule and sticking to it. Break your tasks into smaller, manageable chunks.
What should I do if I feel overwhelmed?
Take a break, talk to someone you trust, and try to focus on one thing at a time.
How can I stay motivated?
Set small goals and reward yourself when you achieve them. Surround yourself with positive influences.
